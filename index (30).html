About DCC
Dominion City Church is a group of people from different backgrounds, denominations, and ethnicities — but we gather in the name of Jesus. We are not perfect people, but we are committed to get it right.

Our Vision: To help people become everything God ordained them to be.

Our Mission: Pray for mankind, prepare believers for their purpose, and propel them forward.
