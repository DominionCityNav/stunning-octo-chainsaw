About Dominion City Church
›
Dominion City Church is a group of people from different backgrounds, denominations, and ethnicities — but we gather in the name of Jesus. We are not perfect people, but we are committed to get it right.

Our Vision: To help people become everything God ordained them to be.

Our Mission: Pray for mankind, prepare believers for their purpose, and propel them forward.

SENIOR / LEAD PASTOR · DOMINION CITY CHURCH
Peace and Blessings to you and your house! You made it — you passed 1,000 churches to get here and we are so glad you stopped by.

My name is Pastor Shan E. Davis and I am honored to serve as the Senior Pastor of Dominion City Church. We are a family of people from different backgrounds, denominations, and ethnicities — but we gather in the name of Jesus. We are not perfect people, but we are committed to get it right.

Our goals are to pursue Godly Maturity, pray for all men, and serve our community. We are empowering individuals to use their God-given right to take authority over every area of their lives — and we are intentionally building a culture of kindness.
Vision & Mission
Our Vision
To help people become everything God has ordained them to be.
Our Mission
Pray for mankind, prepare believers for their purpose, and propel them forward to accomplish their God-given assignments.
